Poll's rules

  • You have 5 minutes after you click a selected poll to answer all the questions
  • If you close the page the time counter doesn't stop. So you can not win additional time by holding the page closed
  • If you load the page again before the time has ended you will have the possibility to continue from where you have stopped
  • You will receive immediate evaluation of your answers when you click the submit button or the time has ended. If the time ends while the page is closed, your results will be evaluated the next time you visit the url again
